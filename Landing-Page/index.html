ABOUT US

We are the best university rugby team in the country and the whole of East Africa as a region as is proven by our many accolades over the years.

Under the leadership of our very able coach Louis Kisia, we have achieved the following:

  • East Africa University 7s Champions
  • 2022 Christie 7s Champions
  • Two time Kenya Cup Semi-Finalists i.e in the 2020/2021 and 2021/2022 seasons

That is more than any other university side in the country.

SCHOLARSHIPS

We believe in the impact that rugby as a sport has on our lives, but being a university rugby team, we put academics above everything else. As Strathmore Leos, we are proud to offer scholarships to excptional rugby players who exemplify excellence not only on the field but also in their academic pursuits. Our program seeks individuals who have demonstrated outstanding rugby skills, sportsmanship and academic achievements in their national examinations.

The scholarship provides financial support, access to professional coaching and academic mentorship, fostering a holistic approach to personal development. We invite aspiring athletes to come train with us and get an opportunity to shape the future of rugby leaders who shine both academically and on the rugby pitch.
